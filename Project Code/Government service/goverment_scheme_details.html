Government Scheme Details

Schemes for Farmers

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

Overview: PM-KISAN provides direct income support to small and marginal farmer families across India to ensure financial stability.

Benefits: Eligible farmers receive ₹6,000 annually in three installments of ₹2,000 each, helping them manage agricultural and household expenses.

Eligibility: Available to all farmer families with cultivable land, excluding institutional landholders and those holding certain government posts or retired employees.

How to Apply? Farmers can register on the PM-KISAN portal (pmkisan.gov.in) with their Aadhaar card, bank details, and land documents, or through the nearest Common Service Center (CSC) or local revenue/agriculture officers.

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Overview: PMFBY is an insurance scheme designed to protect farmers against crop losses due to natural calamities, pests, and diseases.

Benefits: The scheme provides comprehensive coverage for notified crops, ensuring financial support in case of adverse weather conditions and other risks, thereby stabilizing farmers' incomes.

Eligibility: All farmers growing notified crops in notified areas are eligible to enroll in the scheme.

How to Apply? Farmers can enroll through the nearest Common Service Center (CSC), authorized insurance agents, or by contacting their local banks during the designated enrollment periods.

Schemes for Women

Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY)

Overview: PMMVY is a maternity benefit program aimed at providing partial wage compensation to women for wage loss during childbirth and childcare.

Benefits: Under PMMVY, eligible women receive ₹5,000 for their first live birth, disbursed in three installments, to support their health and nutrition during pregnancy and post-delivery.

Eligibility: Pregnant women and lactating mothers having their first live birth are eligible, excluding those employed by the central or state government or public sector undertakings.

How to Apply? Women can register for PMMVY at the nearest Anganwadi center or health facility by providing relevant documents such as Aadhaar card, bank account details, and maternal health records.

Mahila E-Haat

Overview: Mahila E-Haat is an online marketing platform launched to support women entrepreneurs, Self Help Groups (SHGs), and Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) by providing a space to showcase and sell their products and services.

Benefits: This initiative offers an e-platform for women to connect directly with buyers, enhancing their market reach and promoting economic empowerment. It supports various products, including handicrafts, garments, accessories, and more.

Eligibility: Women entrepreneurs, SHGs, and NGOs are eligible to use the platform to promote their products and services.

How to Apply? Eligible participants can register online at the Mahila E-Haat website by providing details about their products or services, along with personal and organizational information. The registration process is user-friendly and accessible to women across India.

Schemes for Students

National Scholarship Portal (NSP)

Overview: The National Scholarship Portal (NSP) is a one-stop platform for various scholarship schemes offered by the Government of India, covering students from Class 1 to PhD.

Benefits: NSP provides financial assistance for tuition fees, maintenance, and other educational expenses, ensuring that deserving students can pursue their studies without financial constraints.

Eligibility: Eligibility criteria vary depending on the specific scholarship scheme, generally based on academic performance, financial need, and other factors.

How to Apply? Students can register and apply online through the NSP portal (scholarships.gov.in) by providing necessary academic and personal details, and uploading required documents.

Mid-Day Meal Scheme

Overview: The Mid-Day Meal Scheme is a school meal program designed to improve the nutritional status of school-age children across India.

Benefits: It provides free lunches on working days for children in primary and upper primary classes in government, government-aided, and local body schools. This ensures that children receive adequate nutrition, which supports their overall development and improves school attendance and performance.

Eligibility: All students enrolled in eligible schools are automatically covered under the scheme.

How to Apply: Enrollment is automatic upon admission to participating schools, with no separate application process required.
